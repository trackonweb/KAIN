Mr. Gaurav Gupta
Mr. Gaurav Kumar Gupta is a visionary with a mission to make water management in India smart and energy efficient. Access to clean water is everyone’s birth right irrespective of who they are and where they are from.
  • He is an electrical engineer from the University of Rajasthan, and MBA in Marketing & Finance from ICFAI, Ahmedabad.
  • He founded Desire Energy Pvt. Ltd in 2011, embarking start of his entrepreneurial journey. Desire Energy is India’s leading water management and efficient energy service company.
  • They provide water supply to more than 5000+ rural areas and 14+ cities through their innovative use of renewable energy, efficient management, and IoT-enabling technology.
Mr. Girish Dangayach
Girish is an outcome driven digital transformation professional with more than 17 years of experience working with leading global companies including KPMG, Accenture, Deloitte, Genpact, and SK Finance.
  • Girish is certified digital strategist from Columbia Business School, and a qualified Chartered Accountant.
  • He has supported many organizations in their transformation journey from beginning of their journey to becoming a unicorn.
Mr. Ravi Khandelwal
Mr. Ravi Khandelwal is a Chartered Accountant by profession, Started his professional journey with Hettich India, Elektrolites (Power), Fortis Healthcare and then creating a brand Yufta.com - A first of its kind fashion experiment.
  • Yufta.com is an exclusive brand that's only available online. Dedicated to the truly original fashion explorer.
  • Mr. Ravi is also an angel investor in ZYOD a fashion brand and also an active member towards the journey of KAIN and its development with a vision to work for its society.
Mrs. Anita Gupta
Mrs. Anita Jasoria is a Social Activist and the Chairperson at Khandelwal Development Forum (KDF) Society, working for community upliftment for the past 15 years.
  • Completed her education with a Bachelor degree in Science (BSc) and a Diploma in Pharmacy.
  • She is also the Chairperson at Government Industrial Training Institute, Thanagazi Dist. (Alwar). Not only this, but she is also a storyteller having her own Youtube Channel.
